How It Works

Balanced View: Shows the overall "vibe" combining news sentiment and thematic categories.

Progress View: Highlights regions with development, health, aid, and positive change stories.

Noise View: Shows areas with conflict, protests, violence, and crisis events.

Data: Real-time GDELT Global Knowledge Graph analysis with H3 spatial indexing.
